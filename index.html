About EduConsult

EduConsult empowers students to make informed career choices in a rapidly changing education and job landscape. With over 50,000+ courses and thousands of colleges available in India, selecting the right path can be overwhelming for students and parents. Research indicates that nearly 60% of students feel uncertain about their career choices after school, often due to lack of reliable guidance and awareness about emerging career opportunities. At EduConsult, we bridge this gap through expert counselling and data-driven insights. We help students identify the right courses, colleges, and career pathways aligned with their interests, strengths, and future industry demands. By combining personalized guidance with knowledge of evolving sectors such as technology, healthcare, management, and emerging fields, we enable students to make confident decisions and build successful careers. EduConsult believes that the right guidance at the right time can shape a lifetime of opportunities.
